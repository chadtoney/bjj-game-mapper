# Executive Summary - BJJ Game Mapper Research

**Date:** February 2026  
**Purpose:** Research-based plan for building a competitive BJJ game mapping application

---

## The Opportunity

The BJJ app market is **fragmented** with no clear market leader. Apps excel in one area but fail to integrate:
- Mapping apps (Grapple Flows, BJJ Graph) lack training tracking
- Tracking apps (BJJ Notes, BJJBuddy) lack visual game mapping  
- Instruction apps (BJJ Fanatics) suffer from technical issues

**Market Size:** 14+ competitor apps analyzed, all with significant user bases but major gaps

---

## Key Findings

### What Works (Keep Doing)
✅ Visual flowcharts and game mapping  
✅ Progress tracking and analytics  
✅ Multimedia integration (videos, photos)  
✅ Clean, intuitive design  
✅ Customization options  

### What Fails (Avoid)
❌ App crashes and data loss (#1 complaint)  
❌ Poor mobile experience  
❌ Steep learning curves  
❌ Aggressive paywalls  
❌ No community/social features  

### Major Market Gaps
🎯 No "Strava for BJJ" (community features)  
🎯 No integrated mapping + tracking solution  
🎯 No reliable, mobile-first experience  
🎯 No apps with excellent onboarding/UX  

---

## Winning Strategy

Build a BJJ app that:
1. **Never crashes or loses data** (reliability first)
2. **Works great on mobile** (mobile-first design)
3. **Combines mapping + tracking** (integrated solution)
4. **Enables community** (sharing, social features)
5. **Easy to learn** (intuitive UX, great onboarding)

---

## MVP Features (Build First)

**Critical 5:**
1. Visual flowchart builder (drag-and-drop, mobile-responsive)
2. Technique library (pre-built positions, custom additions)
3. Personal game map (visualize YOUR game, identify gaps)
4. Reliable sync & offline (never lose data)
5. Intuitive mobile experience (touch-optimized)

**Success Criteria:** 100 users actively mapping their game within 6 months

## Monetization

**Free Tier (Generous):**
- Core game mapping (unlimited)
- Basic technique library  
- Limited training history
- 100MB storage

**Premium ($5-10/month):**
- Advanced analytics
- Unlimited history
- Unlimited storage
- Export features

**Team Tier ($50-100/month):**
- Gym/instructor tools
- Team analytics
- Curriculum sharing

- Core mapping + basic tracking
- **Goal:** 100 active users

- Advanced analytics + mobile apps

- Sharing + social features

### Phase 4: Monetization (4-6 months)

**Total Timeline:** 12-18 months to sustainable business
